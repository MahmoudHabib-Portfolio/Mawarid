    About us

    Mawarid food industry is the Largest specialized olive oil bottling plant in the UAE. The company has a strong capacity of about 300 tons dedicated entirely for olive oil production. n addition, it runs state of the art storing, filtering and bottling facility with special focus on automation and hygiene assuring the highest quality of products.
