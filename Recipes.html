    Hummus

    Hummus is a delicious spread or dip made from chickpeas, tahini, lemon, and spices.

    Ingredients

    1/2 cup of al Ameera Tahina

    14 oz (400g) canned chickpeas, rinsed and drained

    1/4 cup fresh lemon juice

    2 cloves garlic (minced and mashed)

    1/4 cup water

    3/4 to 1 teaspoon salt

    Dressing

    Al Ameera Virgin Olive Oil

    Roasted pine nuts

    Parsley (chopped)

    PREPARATION TIMES

    Total 10 min

    Preparation Time 10 min

    Serving 3 Cups

    DIRECTION

    Blend Al Ameera tahina, chickpeas, lemon juice, garlic and water in a food processor until smooth. Add salt starting at 3/4 teaspoon, to taste. Move to serving dish and add Al Ameera Virgin Olive Oil, pine, and parsley.
